Image Manipulations

A deep dive into the science behind your favorite image manipulation techniques. Ever wonder what the exact math was to increase the contrast of an image? I've made a program which mimics this popular effect using math generated from hours spent researching different imaging tools.

Dartboard Probability Calculations

Who doesn't love math? This dartboard simulation calculates an array of possible outcomes given parameters defined by the user. It can be used in a variety of statistical contexts to generate probability, statisical significance, and standard deviation.

End to End Color-Accurate Imaging

Created a program which reads in the colors of real life objects and displays them on a laptop for categorization and manipulation. If you've ever wanted to know the exact color of your eyes, or if you've wanted to settle a debate about your favorite shirt being more indigo than it is violet, this program is for you.
